About Me

I'm a high school dropout with an M.S. in mathematics and 6 years experience teaching at the college level. While I love teaching and its a very rewarding job, its hasn't been personally challenging in the way I want it to be. I love music (my first passion in life), snowboarding, backpacking (mostly in the mountains) and just about anything having to do with the outdoors. Most of all though, I love solving problems. I love to learn and I love a challenge. Before I finished my degree, I was (among other things) a musician, a journeyman ironworker and a commercial salmon fisherman in Alaska. I think coding is one of the few fields that practically guarantees that I can be a student as long as I want to be.
